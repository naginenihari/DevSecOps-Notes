RCA Analysis
=============
P0, P1, P2, P3, P4, etc.

Every alert is an incident. 

Why that incident came?
Why we were not able to prevent anticipate it?
What is the effect? 
How we cleared?
How can we make sure it will not occur again?
Lessons learnt?

4 Golden Signals
=================
Latency -> How soon our system is responding, low latency
Traffic --> How many requests per min, per hour
Errors -> 500 400 errors
Saturation -> resources like CPU, memory, Disk, Network, etc.
